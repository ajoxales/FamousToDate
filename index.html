Deo Balbuena

Diwata Pares Overload

Deo Balbuena is a celebrated Filipino chef known for his innovative contributions to Filipino cuisine. He is the creative mind behind Diwata Pares Overload, a popular and indulgent variation of the traditional beef pares.

Balbuena's culinary style is characterized by his ability to elevate classic dishes with unique and generous toppings, enhancing both flavor and presentation. His work has garnered a dedicated following and recognition in the culinary community, making him a notable figure in modern Filipino gastronomy.

Peejay Dela Cruz

OTLUM

Have you seen the viral interview of the man who took a cell phone left unattended in a canteen in Ermita, Manila? He was caught in the act via CCTV but he vehemently denied it, reasoning that he merely took what was in front of him.

Netizens began to condemn the man, but things took a turn when someone spotted that the suspect wore a PWD identification card.

The man's name is Peejay Dela Cruz, but he is known online as Otlum, the Tagalog word "multo" (ghost) spelled backwards. In an episode of "Kapuso Mo, Jessica Soho," Otlum shared the story of his life and confirmed that he has a psychosocial disability.

Sixteen Ablero

HIWAGA PARES OVERLOAD

Sixteen "Hiwaga" Ablero is the innovative owner of Hiwaga Pares in Quezon City. He gained fame for his "Hiwaga" overload pares, which features generous portions of two-meat pares with unlimited rice, soup, and drinks for P99.

Starting from a small eatery, Ablero's creative twist on traditional pares quickly attracted a loyal following, allowing him to expand to a larger venue. His success story highlights his dedication to quality and his deep understanding of Filipino comfort food, making Hiwaga Pares a popular and beloved culinary destination.
